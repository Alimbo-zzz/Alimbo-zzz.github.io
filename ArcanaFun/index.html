Welcome to the universe of predictions!

Buy our cards’ unique boosters and get a unique prediction for the day!
We cover all areas of life and try to take a humorous approach: what's best not to eat today?
Who will reciprocate your feelings? An unexpected event! You can learn all this from our daily cards!

How does it work?

You buy our booster with a random card in it.
We'll keep the percentage of one type or another secret for now.
Then you can give the booster to some friend of yours or you can open it yourself. Every card has its own unique message that will cheer you up.
You can collect the cards and build your own happy days calendar or sell/gift them.

Card example:

Gosh, looks like someone’s lucky tonight! It is possible that even if you make yourself look stupid today, there will be someone looking at you in a different way, and you'll never forget those kind eyes.

Roadmap

Our mission

We are a young team of journalists who are really tired of the dullness of everyday life and want to bring some joy to the people.
We do not touch on racial, religious or other discrediting issues.
Our mission is to abstract people from bad moods and hard topics so that
they feel joy for life again.
