  • The ICE Wastelands of Eigu

    The ice wastelands of Eigu are located in the north of the first plane.The extreme cold that prevails in these areas has penetrated sodeeply into the ground that it has begun to provoke strong seismic activity.Because of this, huge faults resembling snow-covered, incredibly deep canyons as wellas smaller faults called ‘cracks’ or ‘crusts’ can be found in the ice wastelands.

    These faults are not just a natural anomaly, but a valuable resource, because they provide coaland titanite* to the few inhabitants of the wastelands. On either side of the largest fault are the astral citiesof Nei Alurg and Tetraglas. They are populated by mechanical scientists, the godless, and exiled mages.

    Far from the faults, only the fittest survive. These are the lands of giants,dangerous predators and creeping plants.

    Wanderes (especially demons) are treated with apprehension in the wastelands,for warm flesh and good equipment make attractive prey.

  • The Keizu Wildlands

    The Keizu Wildlands are the first hunting ground of the first plane forthose who eat like humans (or eat humans)

    The animals and inferior demons who inhabit these places usually possesspowerful limbs to help them traverse great distances and escape predators that hunt themamong the high forests and endless pastures. Among the flora of the wild lands you can findmany aggressive plants, which appeared due to the strong influence of the demons of thesecond plane. The ‘Christian's Bonds’ and the ‘Tears of Villa’ are considered to be especially dangerous.

    It is unknown whether it was the plants that kept the astral cities from filling all possible territory and only letthem locate along the trade route, but the fact remains that in a large area you will not meet many settlers,which is why the lands of Keizu are called ‘Wild’.

  • SUN-SCORCHED LANDS OF TGWA

    It is believed that the Tgwa Desert used to be an incredibly fertile area. Mirinitsa seedlings alone were enoughto provide bread for all the nearby astral cities, including the capital of the first plane, located inthe middle of these lands.

    Due to constant earthquakes and volcanic eruptions of the second plane, the surface began to change.Hills and mountains formed in Tgwa (the very centre of the first plane) and all the big rivers merged intoone, starting to form a huge spiral around it. The heat emanating from beneath the earth dried upthe underground waters. The fertile soil turned into desert, the fields into dunes, and the beautiful forestsinto terrible trees warped by the drought.

    Now only madmen come back here, apart from travelers who strive to conquer the black castleand find a way into the second plane.

    In the immense heat, only the fittest survive, and that’s what makes the locals some of themost dangerous inhabitants of Pandemonium.

about us

We are a young team united by common interests and game development, with rich experience in the mobile market. Our project is a fantasy meta–universe with references to things we all love, bulit on a blockchain system using original NFT cards.

our story

In June 2021, a group of programmers and designers already working on mobile games turn their eyes on the blockchain system. It turned out that all of us bought NFT cards for collectible or blockchain games. Games cannot develop without technology, and technology is well promoted through games. We have all the potential to create new interesting products with the lastest technology.

our mission

The priority for us is to use and popularize modern technologies and create games. We believe that the blockchain system is the future.
